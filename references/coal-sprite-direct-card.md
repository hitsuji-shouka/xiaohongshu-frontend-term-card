每页提示词必须包含：

```text
3:4 vertical portrait, 1080 x 1440, finished Xiaohongshu knowledge card,
original black fuzzy coal-sprite protagonist with short charcoal bristles,
two large off-white circular eyes and tiny thin limbs,
render every supplied Chinese label and short code exactly as written,
medium-high information density, 60%-75% useful content area,
no stick figure, no empty boxes, no placeholder lines, no cropped or overlapping text.
```

卡片必须同时有标题、真实用户界面/行为、3-5 个解释模块和一句结论。模型文字有错时优先整页重生。
